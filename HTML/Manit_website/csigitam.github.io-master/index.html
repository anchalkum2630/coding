Web Development Club
The Web Development Club is composed of students interested in the Web Programming and Development. We are currently exploring projects to expand students' capabilities in web development. Students will learn more on new age web technologies.
Design
The Design Club projects challenge students to explore their creativity and critical-thinking skills while pushing them to try a range of techniques and media.Learning about design as a way of thinking.
Cultural Club
It is the place where students can showcase what they have got. It helps to discover their talent. Cultural events include competitions such as instrumental, singing, dance etc. Every year this club organizes activities

MCA Association is a body incubated by Department of Computer Application, MANIT Bhopal. We work to build and manage the Alumni Network as well as guide the current students at MCA towards the achievement of their goals. We house two clubs, Matrix and Rubiks. The first one is the technical club while the later is the cultural club. As their name suggest, we work with these two clubs to bring better opportunities to the students and enhance their technical as well as non technical skills.
